Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our blockchain voting system.

How secure is the voting system?

The system uses end-to-end encryption, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and tamper-proof blockchain storage to ensure that votes are secure, anonymous, and immutable. Each vote is cryptographically signed and recorded on a distributed ledger that's verified by multiple nodes.

Who can access the platform?

Only authorized organizations can access the platform. Once onboarded, the admin generates voter credentials using employee or student IDs. Voters receive unique credentials that allow them to participate in specific elections.

How do voters cast their votes?

Voters receive login credentials from their admin. Once logged in, they verify their identity through multi-factor authentication and securely cast their vote using our intuitive voting interface. The system provides a confirmation receipt that doesn't reveal voting choices.

Can I see the results after voting?

Yes! Once voting ends, results are publicly available on the results page. Every voter can also verify that their vote was counted — without revealing their identity — through their unique voting receipt that references their anonymized blockchain transaction.

Does this platform comply with data privacy laws?

Absolutely. The system is designed to comply with GDPR, ISO 27001, and other international data protection and information security standards. We implement privacy by design principles throughout our architecture.

What if someone tries to vote more than once?

That's not possible. The system enforces strict one-person-one-vote logic using blockchain-backed smart contracts and voter authentication controls. Each verified identity can only cast one vote per election, and attempts to vote multiple times are automatically blocked.
